Products and Ongoing Work

Here you can find the products and ideas I am building, with a focus on safer communication, emotional well-being, and thoughtful system design.

Current Projects

These projects reflect my interest in building systems that help people feel safer, more supported, and less burdened in digital environments.

Available

Kotoba Mimamori

A web tool that helps users review text before posting by making communication risks more visible. It is designed as a gentle pause before sending words into public spaces.

In Development

Safe Comment Filter

A moderation support concept designed to reduce emotional harm when reviewing abusive or harmful comments, especially in creator-oriented environments such as video platforms.

Planned

Sensitive Image Protection

A concept aimed at reducing emotional harm caused by unexpected disturbing images in online communication, especially in direct or semi-private channels.

Ongoing

Portfolio and Supporting Resources

In addition to product development, I also maintain practical resources, notes, and supporting pages that help explain the ideas, intentions, and background behind my work.

Development Approach

I do not aim only for technical correctness. I also care about how a product feels when someone actually uses it.

I value systems that are understandable, calm, practical, and sustainable. My goal is not simply to add features, but to reduce harm, lower friction, and create tools that people can continue using with confidence.
