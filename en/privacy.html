Privacy Policy

This page explains how inquiry information and related data are handled on this portfolio site.

1. Basic Policy

This portfolio site respects the privacy of visitors and aims to handle personal information in an appropriate and careful manner.

Information submitted through the contact form will be used only within the scope necessary to respond to inquiries, maintain the site, and improve safety and operational quality.

2. Information That May Be Collected

This site may collect the following information when you use the contact form or related features:

  • Name or handle name
  • Email address
  • Inquiry category
  • Message content
  • Technical data necessary for spam prevention and site security

3. Purpose of Use

The information collected on this site may be used for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries and related communication
  • Confirming the content and context of inquiries
  • Preventing spam, abuse, or harmful submissions
  • Maintaining and improving the safety and operation of the site

4. Third-Party Services

This site may use third-party services for purposes such as analytics, spam prevention, hosting, and form processing.

For example, the site may use services such as Google Analytics, Google reCAPTCHA, Amazon Web Services, API Gateway, Lambda, and Amazon SES as part of its technical operation.

5. Cookies and Access Analysis

This site may use cookies or similar technologies in order to understand usage patterns, improve site quality, and maintain operational stability.

Information collected through analytics tools does not aim to directly identify individual visitors.

6. Management of Inquiry Information

Inquiry information is handled with reasonable care and only to the extent necessary for communication, security, and site operation.

However, please avoid sending highly sensitive personal information unless it is truly necessary.

7. Disclosure to Third Parties

Personal information will not be disclosed or provided to third parties without the individual’s consent, except where disclosure is required by law or otherwise necessary under applicable rules.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed in response to changes in site content, services, technical operation, or legal requirements.

The latest version published on this site shall apply.

9. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please use the contact page.
